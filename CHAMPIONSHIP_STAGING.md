# Championship Time Standards — STAGING DOCUMENT

**Purpose:** Real 2026 qualifying times for the 5 championship tiers shown on
the profile Championship Standards table. Chase verifies here before data
moves into `src/lib/standards.js`.

**Tiers covered (in order from easiest → hardest):**
1. TAGS — Texas Age Group Swimming Championships (14 & Under only)
2. Sectionals — national max standard (same for all ages)
3. Futures — USA Swimming Futures Championships (18 & Under)
4. Junior Nationals — Speedo Junior National Championships (18 & Under qualifying, not bonus)
5. Nationals — Toyota National Championships (18 & Under)

**Source:** Official 2026 USA Swimming + Texas Swimming Association PDFs.

**Notes:**
- Relays excluded (200 FR-R, 400 FR-R, 200 MED-R, 400 MED-R, 800 FR-R)
- Bonus standards excluded — only qualifying standards
- Asterisks (*) and plus (+) markers from the source are footnote markers, stripped
- "x" in source means the event is not offered at that tier for that age — recorded as "—"
- TAGS only covers 10&U, 11-12, 13-14. 15-16 and 17-18 athletes do NOT see a TAGS column.

## BEHAVIOR RULES FOR THE UI

- **TAGS column**: visible only for athletes in 10 & Under, 11-12, or 13-14
  age groups. Hidden for 15-16 and 17-18.
- **Sectionals / Futures / Jr Nats / Nationals**: always visible.
- **"x" entries** (marked as "—" in tables above) render as "—" in the
  Championship Standards cell — that tier doesn't offer that event for
  that age group.
- **Course-specific events**: 500/1000/1650 Free are SCY only. 400/800/1500
  Free are LCM only. Never render cross-course.
