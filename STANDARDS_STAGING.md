# USA Swimming Motivational Standards — STAGING DOCUMENT

**Purpose:** Every time Chase sends official USA Swimming standards screenshots,
the values are transcribed here FIRST for verification, THEN moved into
`src/lib/standards.js`. Two-step process prevents transcription errors from
hitting the codebase.

**Scope:** SCY and LCM only. SCM is skipped per product decision — SCM meets are
rare for Confluence Swim's athletes.

**Source:** USA Swimming 2024-2028 Motivational Standards (official published
document). Relays (200 FR-R, 200 MED-R) are excluded — the standards table is
for individual events only.

**Format:** Times below are the raw times as they appear in the USA Swimming
document. Asterisks (*) in the source are footnote markers and not transcribed.

## VERIFICATION PROCESS

1. Chase sends screenshots for an age group
2. Claude transcribes into the table above for that age group
3. Chase reviews the transcription against the original screenshots
4. On Chase's "verified" confirmation, Claude moves the data into
   `src/lib/standards.js` as executable code
5. Screenshot can be deleted; staging document serves as archive

## KNOWN EDGE CASES

- **100 IM only exists in SCY.** LCM has no 100 IM event.
- **SCY distance freestyle: 500 / 1000 / 1650.** LCM distance freestyle:
  400 / 800 / 1500.
- **10 & Under does NOT include 400 IM.** Older age groups do.
- **10 & Under does NOT include 1000 Free / 1650 Free (SCY) or 800 Free
  / 1500 Free (LCM).** Older age groups do.
